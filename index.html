The heart behind the work

A lifelong love of family history

Anna inherited her love of family history from her Grandma Emma Adams. Her passion for history, teaching, and family came together in a simple idea: learn history backwards by discovering how world events shaped our own ancestors’ lives.

As a mother of six, grandmother of 21, and great-grandmother of four, Anna is building History, Family, and Me to help families preserve more than records—to preserve belonging.
