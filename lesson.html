Story Time

✓

A family tree is a special picture that shows the people in your family and how you are all connected.

You are like a little seed at the bottom. Your parents helped you grow. Their parents—your grandparents—helped them grow. And long, long ago, your great-grandparents started your family story!

When we learn about the people who came before us, we discover where we get our courage, our smiles, and even our favorite foods. That’s the magic of family history!
